Why Work With Me

All problems have solutions. Allow me to help you find them.

Before I tell you about my journey, let me ask: do you recognise yourself in this situation?

The pressure of tools breaking, deadlines closing in, and survival-mode fixes just about keeping things afloat. You make it work, no contracts breached, no disasters, but every shortcut adds to the growing backlog labelled tech debt.

That was me, for over fourteen years.

Then I asked myself a simple question: what if we stopped firefighting and started building smarter?

That is the space I work in now.

I help teams get more from the tools, systems, and processes they already have by building scalable, integrated solutions across the Atlassian ecosystem. I focus on connecting Jira Software, Jira Service Management, and Confluence in ways that simplify complexity and deliver lasting efficiency and impact.

And yes, I use AI confidently. The right technology does not replace us, it helps us stay objective, unlock ideas, remove bottlenecks, and free up time for the work that matters most.

I am not a one-man band, but part of a modern, lean, and smart setup.

About LT.Solutions

Hands-on Atlassian expertise, designed to scale with knowledge that stays in your team.

LT.Solutions is the consultancy I built to deliver exactly that: smarter, scalable solutions built on the full Atlassian platform. I work with medium to large organisations to modernise service delivery, improve collaboration, and unlock real value from Jira Software, Jira Service Management, and Confluence.

My focus is technical and hands-on: robust architecture, clean configurations, automation, and clear documentation. Every project prioritises knowledge transfer so your teams are not left dependent on me. Instead, they leave confident, skilled, and self-sufficient.
