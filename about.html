Our Foundation

Where technology meets clarity

LT.Solutions was built on the belief that great systems do more than run efficiently, they help people think clearly.

Every project starts with a balance of structure and adaptability, where technology supports clarity, not the other way around. We combine the rigour of Atlassian architecture with a human approach that values learning, trust, and progress that lasts.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we build

These values shape every conversation, every deliverable, and every decision we make with our clients. They are how we measure ourselves and how we make sure our work creates real impact.

Integrity

We speak plainly, even when it is uncomfortable. Honest advice builds trust faster than flattery. Every recommendation is grounded in what serves your long term success.

Collaboration

We do not consult and disappear. We work shoulder to shoulder with your teams, listening, teaching, and building together. Collaboration means shared ownership of both the problem and the solution.

Clarity

Complex systems deserve simple language. We turn technical noise into transparency so everyone, from service desk agent to CIO, understands how things connect.

Impact

We measure our work in outcomes, not hours. The goal is not to hand over documentation, it is to leave measurable improvements: faster response times, cleaner workflows, and more confident teams.

Continuity

Our success is when you no longer need us. Every framework, automation, and guide is designed to make your teams self reliant. We build systems that outlive our involvement. Capability that endures.

Partnership Philosophy

Values we bring, and hope you do too

Partnership works best when both sides value honesty, curiosity, and respect for craft. We bring depth of experience and transparent communication, and we look for clients who bring openness, accountability, and a genuine desire to improve. When both sides share those values, transformation moves faster and the results stick.

Why Work With Me

A quick personal note from Andreas.

All problems have solutions. Allow me to help you find them.

Before I tell you about my journey, let me ask: do you recognise yourself in this situation?

The pressure of tools breaking, deadlines closing in, and survival-mode fixes just about keeping things afloat. You make it work, no contracts breached, no disasters, but every shortcut adds to the growing backlog labelled tech debt.

That was me, for over fourteen years.

Then I asked myself a simple question: what if we stopped firefighting and started building smarter?

That is the space I work in now.

I help teams get more from the tools, systems, and processes they already have by building scalable, integrated solutions across the Atlassian ecosystem. I focus on connecting Jira Software, Jira Service Management, and Confluence in ways that simplify complexity and deliver lasting efficiency and impact.

And yes, I use AI confidently. The right technology does not replace us, it helps us stay objective, unlock ideas, remove bottlenecks, and free up time for the work that matters most.

I am not a one-man band, but part of a modern, lean, and smart setup.
